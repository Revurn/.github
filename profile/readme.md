# Welcome to Revurn!

Revurn brings together game discovery, unified libraries, and safe social features all in one modern platform.

Revurn is currently being developed by **a single developer**, so new features and releases may take time — thank you for your patience and support!

Whether you're here to play, publish, or connect, you’re in the right place!

# What is Revurn?

Revurn is a website designed to create a seamless ecosystem for gamers and developers.

## For Gamers
- Discover new indie titles through the **Revurn Store**  
- Access your games from your **Revurn Library**  
- Connect third-party stores like **Steam** and **Epic Games** to unify your entire library in one place  
- Enjoy discord-like social features:  
  - **DMs** — Safe, text-only direct messaging  
  - **Realms** — Server-like community spaces for groups, friends, and communities  

## For Developers
- Upload your games to Revurn **completely for free**  
- Reach new players through the Revurn Store  
- Enjoy a safe upload environment with human-reviewed content before publishing  
- Be part of a growing platform built with indie developers in mind  

Revurn’s mission is to create a modern, safe, and community-driven space for both players and creators — built step-by-step by one dedicated developer.
